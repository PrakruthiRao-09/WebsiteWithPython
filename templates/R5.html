My Favorite Sandwich

This is my very favorite sanwich recipe! Hope you like it! - Prakruthi R.

Ingredients

2 slices of bread of choice ( I used sourdough)

1 ripe tomato, sliced (I use beefsteak)

2 slices swiss, mozzarella, or mild cheddar cheese

2-3 pieces of fresh lettuce ( I reccomend either romaine or butterhead)

1 teaspoon mayo

pinch of pepper

Instructions

1. Place the bread on a pan greased lightly with butter on medium heat and cook for 3-5 minutes or until toasted golden brown (see note)

2. Let the bread rest until cool to the touch, and then spread both slices with the small amount of mayo, as well as a sprinkle of pepper

3. Add the two slices of cheese of your choice.

4. Add the peices of lettuce on top of the cheese.

5. Top with as many slices of tomato you like, and place the other slice of bread on top.

6. Enjoy!



Note - If wanted, you don't have to toast the bread! just skip the first step and do the rest
