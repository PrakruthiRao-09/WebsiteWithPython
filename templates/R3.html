No-Churn Raspberry Ice cream recipe

Ingredients

2 cups heavy cream

2 cups of fresh/frozen raspberries

1/4 cups of fine/caster sugar

1 can condensed milk

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

pinch of salt

Instructions

1. Set stove to medium heat and place raspberries and sugar into saucepan. Simmer and stir for around 5 or 10 minutes, until sugar has melted and raspberries have come to a mush

push mixture through a seive to remove seeds and large bits of pulp. Small bits of pulp are ok. Set aside to cool.

2. Using a hand-held mixer or stand mixer, beat the cream to stiff peaks

3. In a separate bowl stir together condensed milk, vanilla, and salt. Gently fold in the whipped cream.

4. Pour the cream mixture into a loaf pan or any 9x5 inch container.

5. Take the cooled raspberry mixture as well as a spoon and toothpick

6. Take the spoon and plop dollopfuls in various places on top of the cream mixture. Use the tooth-pick and swirl to create a pattern of choice

7. Cover tightly, and chill in freezer overnight or until solidified

Enjoy!
