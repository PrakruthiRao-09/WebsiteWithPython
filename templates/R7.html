5-minute Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients

1/2 cup cooked pasta of choice (I like to use either macaroni, shells, or farfalle(bowtie))

1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded

1/4 cup cheddar cheese, shredded

1/4 cup whole milk

pinch of salt

sprinkle of pepper

Instructions

1. Take pasta, cheeses, and milk and combine in a microwave safe bowl.

2.Add salt and pepper and mix well

3. Cover lightly in micrawave safe plastic wrap and heat in microwave on high for 3 minutes

4. Mix well, and then microwave once again for an aditional 2 minutes

5. Stir well until smooth, and add whichever mix-ins you please

6. Enjoy!
